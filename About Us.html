About Us

Introduction

Our school is talent-oriented and enrolls both female and male students. Additionally, the school provides a conductive environment for various sports where students, besides their studiess, can engage in other activities as they are taught various skills such as cookey, welding, football, netball, and swimming

Location; our school found in Dar es Salaam Region, at Ilala District, Area Gerezani, Address P.O.Box 2020, Dar es Salaam.


We welcome you to receive high-quality services for your child's development and a top-tier education.

School History

Our school was founded in 1948 and was originally known as Pugu College. The institution underwent various changes the supervision of the ministry of Education until july 1,2023, when it began operating officially as a government school. It received registration from NACTVET(No.S.846/S1045) and has been a NACTVET-registered school for approximately three years.

The school provides secondary education from form One to Form Six, Offering both Science and Arts stream
